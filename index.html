Six Checkpoints

Between you and a bad car.

Auction economics. Profitability math. Hard gates. Carfax / AutoCheck verification. Demand-matching against your current lot. Full financial analysis with exit planning. Each stage talks to the others — a finding in stage four changes the math in stage six. No other tool does this.

Exit Path

Before you bid, not after.

You see two paths before you spend a dollar: what it retails for on your lot — turn time, margin estimate — and what you walk away with if retail doesn't work. Your ICO floor. Your MMR wholesale. Your capital-at-risk number. You know the downside before you commit.

Real Money
