Six-Stage Pipeline

One verdict per car — six AI stages deep.

Every vehicle passes through six AI-driven stages before Vintryx recommends it. Auction economics. Profitability math. Hard gates. Vehicle history verification. Demand matching against your current inventory. Full financial analysis with exit planning. Each stage talks to the others — a finding in stage four changes the math in stage six. No other tool does this — most just show you a price and a condition report.

Exit Path

Two paths, before you bid.

Before you spend a dollar, Vintryx shows you two paths: what this car retails for on your lot with an expected turn time and margin estimate, and what you walk away with if retail doesn't work — your ICO floor, your MMR wholesale value, your capital-at-risk number. You know the upside and the downside before you raise your hand.

Real Money

A real cash offer on every car you own.

Vintryx puts a real cash offer on every vehicle in your inventory. Not an estimate. Not a "market value." An actual offer backed by real capital. If a car is retailing below its Vintryx ICO, you're losing money every day you hold it. The system tells you that — and it tells you what to do about it.

Condition Score

Built to flag what the auction misses.

Auction condition reports miss things. Vintryx runs its own AI analysis — vehicle images, history data, and proprietary scoring — to produce a condition confidence score that's independent from what the auction tells you. When the auction says "clean" and the data says "suspicious," Vintryx flags the gap.
