---

## Tone Rules

- **Write as a peer to dealers, not down to them.** "The mindset and process the top dealer groups have always used" credits the audience — it doesn't position Vintryx as the savior.
- **Concrete over abstract.** *"See the math, see the risk, see the exit"* beats *"make data-driven decisions."*
- **Anti-dashboard.** Always frame Vintryx against generic data tools. We are *not* a dashboard. We are a discipline.
- **Never use "AI" as the hero.** AI is the engine; the discipline is the product.
